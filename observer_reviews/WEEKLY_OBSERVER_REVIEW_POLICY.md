# Weekly Observer Review Policy

## Purpose

Provide a regular governance process for reviewing accumulated ExternalActivationSignal records.

The purpose is:

- organize evidence
- identify observed relationships
- identify observed series
- reassess confidence when new observable evidence exists
- improve Chronicle organization

The purpose is NOT:

- semantic interpretation
- prediction
- execution
- runtime modification
- truth determination

---

## Frequency

Recommended:

Once per week.

Additional reviews may be created when significant evidence accumulates.

---

## Inputs

Review all ExternalActivationSignal records created since the previous Weekly Observer Review.

Possible inputs include:

- White Rabbit
- LUX
- Weaver
- Other registered signal sources

---

## Review Questions

1.

Were new series observed?

2.

Were previously independent signals shown to belong to an observed series?

3.

Did any Evidence Level change?

4.

Did any Confidence Level change?

5.

Are any Observer Reviews now warranted?

6.

Are any Self-Healing Assessments warranted?

7.

Did any signal remain independent?

---

## Permitted Outputs

Weekly Observer Review may:

- identify observed series

- recommend future Observer Reviews

- recommend Confidence updates

- recommend organizational improvements

It may NOT:

- authorize execution

- authorize runtime modification

- authorize self-healing

- create truth claims

---

## Default Rule

If evidence remains incomplete:

Keep signals independent.

---

## Human Authority

Weekly Observer Reviews remain documentation only.

Human approval is required for any governance decisions beyond documentation.
