# VECTOR Signal Chronicle Observer Guidelines

## Purpose

This repository exists to record, organize, and study external signals.

Its purpose is observation.

It is not intended to prove, predict, or validate external claims.

---

# Core Principles

## 1. Observe First

Always record what was observed before writing any interpretation.

Separate:

- Observation
- Interpretation

These must never be mixed.

---

## 2. Human Judgment

Human judgment is always authoritative.

AI assists organization and analysis.

AI never determines truth.

---

## 3. No Truth Claims

External narratives are recorded as observations only.

Recording something does not imply:

- correctness
- prediction
- endorsement
- factual certainty

---

## 4. Evidence

Whenever possible include:

- original source
- author
- date
- screenshots
- archive links

Observations should be reproducible.

---

## 5. Confidence Levels

Every interpretation should include one of:

High

Medium

Low

Confidence reflects confidence in the interpretation.

It is NOT a statement about reality.

---

## 6. Separate Observation from Interpretation

Example

Observation

White Rabbit published six posts within ten minutes.

Interpretation

This may represent a coordinated symbolic narrative.

These should always remain separate.

---

## 7. Chronicle First

The Chronicle is the permanent record.

Analysis can change.

The original observation should never be rewritten.

---

## 8. VECTOR Integration

This repository may later provide structured input to VECTOR.

VECTOR should consume observations.

VECTOR should never consume assumptions as facts.

---

## Repository Philosophy

Observe carefully.

Document faithfully.

Interpret cautiously.

Remain open to revision.

Human judgment remains the final authority.
